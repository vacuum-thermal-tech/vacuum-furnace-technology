# Vacuum Furnace Guide and Technology

Vacuum furnace technology is widely used in aerospace, automotive, and advanced materials industries. It enables clean, oxidation-free heat treatment and high-purity melting processes.

Learn more about vacuum furnace technology and industrial applications.

## Types of Vacuum Furnaces

Common vacuum furnace equipment includes:

* Vacuum induction melting furnace (VIM)
* Vacuum brazing furnace
* Vacuum heat treatment furnace
* High temperature vacuum furnace
